间。人们在长期存活对象的实验中发现[13],肺动脉应用连续吻合技术可造成吻合口狭窄,因此,本组应用了间断吻合技术,为下一步的深入研究提供技术基础。总之,本研究成功地建立了无心跳供体肺移植的实验模型,而且这一模型是极具应用价值的。同时,本实验结果显示,无心跳供体应用于肺移植是安全而有效的。大白鼠NHBD肺的热缺血时间(WIT)安全时限为30min,当WIT为60min时,移植后的NHBD肺结构和功能损害较为明显,不适合于肺移植。当然,本实验仅是动物实验,应用到临床还有许多问题,也没有这方面的报道。而且,尚有如下问题亟待解决:(1)心跳停止前尸体供体给予肝素化在临床上很难做到。(2)多数中心对术后较远期(至少1个月以上)存活的效果并未进行深入探讨。(3)大白鼠供肺热缺血期间,其萎陷状态下的WIT期限及移植效果尚不得而知。这些将在今后的工作中继续进行。参考文献1BrighamKL.Mechanismsoflunginjury.ClinChestMed,1982,3:8.2LechnerJF,StonerGD,YoakumGH,etal.Invitrocarcinogenesisstudieswithhumantracheobronchialtissuesandcells.In:SchiffLJ,ed.Invitromodelsofrespiratoryepithelium.BocaRaton,FL:CRCPress,1986,143-159.3UmemeoriY,DateH,UnoK,etal.Improvedlungfunctionbyuroki-naseinfusionincaninelungtransplantationusingnon-heart-beatingdonors.AnnThoracSurg,1995,59:1513-1518.4WatanabeA,KawaharadaN,KusajimaK,etal.Influenceofoxygenininflationgasduringlungischemiaonischemia-reperfusioninjury.JThoracCardiovascSurg,1997,114:332-338.5HaniudaM,HasegawaS,ShiraishiT,etal.Themaintenanceofaerobicmetabolismduringlungpreservation.Effectsofinflationvolumeduringlungpreservationonpulmonarycapillarypermeability.JThoracCar-diovascSurg,1996,112:85-93.6GoldblumSE,WuKM,JayM,etal.Lungmydloperoxidaseasamea-sureofpulmonaryleukostasisinrabbits.JApplPhysiol,1985,59:1978-1985.7MarshallJM.Vertebrateandsmoothmuslce.In:MountcastleVB,Medicalphysiology.Vol1.14thed,1980,120.8YamazakiR,YokomiseH,KeshavjeeSH,etal.ThesuperiorityofanextracellularfluidsolutionoverEuro-Collinssolutionforpulmonarypreservation.Transplantation,1990,49:690-694.9Keshavjee.SH,Yamazaki.MSc,Yokomise.H,etal.Theroleofdex-tran40andpotassiuminextendedhypothermiclungpreservationfortransplantation.JThoracCardiovascSurg,1992,103:314-325.10PeggDE.Organpreservation.SurgClinNorthAm,1986,66:617.11杨秀滨,吴清玉,唐承君,等.Euro-Collins液加前列腺素E1与低钾右旋糖酐液低温保存供体兔肺18h的实验研究.中国循环杂志,1996,11:688-691.12MiyoshiS,ShimokawaS,SchreinemakersH,etal.ComparisonoftheUniversityofWisconsinpreservationsolutionandothercrystalloidper-fusatesina30-hourrabbitlungpreservationmodel.J

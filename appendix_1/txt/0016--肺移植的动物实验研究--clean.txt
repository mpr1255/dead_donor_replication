好;支气管吻合口不漏气,不狭窄及肺膨胀好;肺顺应性好;移植肺通气及换气功能好;术后动物存活,能站立走动。笔者采取以下措施达到上述标准:(1)血管及支气管吻合技术熟练;(2)切取供肺、受体肺及植入肺过程中均用肝素;在灌注供肺动脉液中及保存供肺液中均加入肝素,故移植后病理检查肺血管内均无血栓;(3)移植手术中动作轻柔,不挤压肺;(4)严格控制输液,本组静脉输液量10ml·kg-1·h-1,麻醉药液体量2~3ml·kg-1·h-1;(5)供肺修剪要合乎要求,要有与肺静脉相连的左房袖,较长的肺总动脉,适当长的一侧总支气管,边缘要平整光滑。(本文承蒙福建医科大学附属协和医院病理科大力支持,致衷心感谢。)参考文献:[1]丁嘉安,周伯年,姜格宁,等.二种不同成分灌洗液肺保存效果的对比[J].中华器官移植杂志,1999,20:53~55.[2]罗方俊,陈胜喜,蒋海河,等.缺血预处理对犬移植肺保存效果的研究[J].湖南医科大学学报,1997,22:195~198.AnExperimentalStudyofLungTransplantationZHOULun,CHENChun,JINYou-song,etal(DepartmentofThoracicSurgery,TheAffiliatedUnionHospital,FujianMedicalUniversity,Fuzhou350001,China)ABSTRACT:ObjectiveToobservethefunctionoftransplantedlungandpostoperativerestorationinexperimentalanimals.MethodsFourleftlungautotransplantationandfourleftlungallotransplantationwereperformedondogsandcows.ResultsAftertransplantation,thefunctionsofventilationandgastransferinthetransplantedlungwerenormal.Theanimalsrestoredspontaneousrespiration.Thedogscouldstandupandwalk.ConclusionThisexperimentalmethodsprovideanoperativeprocedureoflungtransplantationonanimal,andcouldbeapreparatorystepforfurtherclinicallungtransplantationpractice.KEYWORDS:animal,laboratory;lungtransplantation6JournalofFujianMedicalUniversityVol.35No.1Mar,2001

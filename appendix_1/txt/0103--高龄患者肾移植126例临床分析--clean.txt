疗;另一方面术后应尽可能使用以Wu-T3、Zenapax和Simulect等抗T淋巴细胞的单克隆抗体为代表的新型免疫抑制剂,并使用以霉酚酸酯代替硫唑嘌呤的新型免疫抑制方案。[参考文献][1]VivasCA,HickeyDP,JordanML,etal.Renaltransplantationinpatients65yearsoldorolder[J].JUrol,1992,147:990.[2]IsmailN,HakinRM,HheldermanJH,etal.Renalreplacementtherapiesintheelderly:partⅡ[J].AMJKidneyDisease,1994,23:1.[3]LundgrenG,FehrmanI,GunnarssonB,etal.Cadavericrenaltransplanationinpatientsover55yearsofagewithspecialemphasisonimmunosuppressivetherapy[J].TransplantProc,1994,74:602-604[4]OjoA,Meier-KriescheH,HansonJA,etal.Mycophenolatemofetilreduceslaterenalallograftlossindependentofacuterejection[J].Transplantation,2000,69:2405.[5]JordanML,NovickAC,SteinmullerD,etal.Renaltransplanta-tionintheoldrecipient[J].JUrol,1985,134:243-246.[6]石炳毅.抗IL-2R单克隆抗体———Simulect[J].中华器官移植杂志,1999,20:242-243.[7]NashanB,LightS,HardieIR,etal.Reductionofacuterenalallo-graftrejectionbydaclizumab[J].Transplantation,1999,67:110.[8]KahanBD,RajaagopalanPR,HallM.Reductionoftheoccurrenceofacutecellularrejectionamongrenalallograftrecipientstreatedwithbasiliximab,achimericanti-interleukin-2-receptormonoclonalantibody[J].Transplantation,1999;67:276.(2002-12-24收稿;2003-03-15修回)1372003年6月成都医药Jun.2003第29卷第3期CHENGDUMEDICALJOURNALVol.29No.3

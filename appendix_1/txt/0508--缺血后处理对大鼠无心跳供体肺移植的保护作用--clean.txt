少，说明缺血后处理可通过抑制细胞凋亡来减轻肺移植术后缺血再灌注损伤。虽然众多研究表明细胞凋亡与再灌注损伤的形成密切相关，而缺血后处理能通过抑制细胞凋亡发挥保护作用，但其具体机制还需要进一步的研究。参考文献1.DeOliveiraNC,OsakiS,MaloneyJD,etal.Lungtransplantationwithdonationaftercardiacdeathdonors:long–termfollow–upinasinglecenter[J].JThoracCardiovascSurg,2010,139(5):1306–1312.2.MasonDP,ThuitaL,AlsterJM,etal.Shouldlungtransplantationbeperformedusingdonationaftercardiacdeath?TheUnitedStatesexperience[J].JThoracCardiovascSurg,2008,136(4):1061–1066.3.张志,郭永庆.无心跳供体肺移植研究进展[J].北京医学,2010,32(7)：565–568.ZHANGZhi,GUOYongqing.Researchprogressinlungtransplantationwithdonationaftercardiacdeathdonor[J].BeijingMedicine,2010,32(7):565–568.4.ZhaoZQ,CorveraJS,HalkosME,etal.Inhibitionofmyocardialinjurybyischemicpost–conditioningduringreperfusion:comparisonwithischemicpreconditioning[J].AmJPhysiolHeartCircPhysiol,2003,285(2):579–588.5.ZhaoZQ.Postconditioninginreperfusioninjury:Astatusreport[J].CardiovascDrugsTher,2010,24(3):265–279.6.胡庆华,陈胜喜,罗凡砚,等.大鼠无心跳供体肺移植尸体肺的保护方法[J].中南大学学报:医学版,2008,33(4)：353–358.HUQinghua,CHENShengxi,LUOFanyan,etal.Protectionofcadaverlungsofnon–heart–beatingdonorlungtransplantationinrats[J].JournalofCentralSouthUniversity.MedicalScience,2008,33(4):353–358.7.张新,乔晨晖,郅兴义,等.两套管一支架管大鼠肺移植模型探讨[J].医学论坛杂志,2006,26(17):25–28.ZHANGXin,QIAOChenhui,ZHIXingyi,etal.Two–cuff–one–stenttechniqueforratorthotopicpulmonarytransplantation[J].JournalofMedicalForum,2006,26(17):25–28.8.HausenB,BahraM,MuellerP,etal.Donorpretreatmentwithambroxolordexamethasonefailstoamelioratereperfusioninjuryinexperimental缺血后处理对大鼠无心跳供体肺移植的保护作用胡庆华，等389lungtransplantation[J].TranspInt,1998,11(3):186–194.9.龚勇泉,李劲松,王建军.肺移植四十年进展[J].国外医学移植和血液净化分册,2005,3(2):15–18.GONGYongquan,LIJingsong,WANGJianjun.Advancementinlungtransplantationforthepastfortyyears[J].ForeignMedicalSciencesSectionofTransplantationandHemopurification,2005,3(2):15–18.10.蒋海河,陈胜喜,罗万俊,等.缺血预处理对犬肺移植供肺的保护作用[J].中国器官移植杂志,1998,19(3):154–156.JIANGHaihe,CHENShengxi,LUOWanjun,etal.Protectiveeffectsofpreconditioningondonorlungincaninelungtransplantation[J].ChineseJ

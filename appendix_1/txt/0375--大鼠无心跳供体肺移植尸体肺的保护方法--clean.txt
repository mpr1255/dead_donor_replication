肺损伤更为严重。因此要使NHBD肺移植获得成功,必须采取一定的保护措施,更好地保存尸体肺的结构和功能。此外,本次实验建立的大鼠无心跳供体左肺原位移植模型为“两套管一支架法”,只能用作短期观察,不能观察大鼠移植术后长期生存情况,因此得出的结论还有待进一步实验验证。参考文献:[1]KutschkaI,SommerSP,HohlfeldJM,etal.In-situtopi-calcoolingoflunggrafts:earlygraftfunctionandsurfactantanalysisinaporcinesinglelungtransplantmodel[J].EurJCardiothoracSurg,2003,24(3):411-419.[2]MakioH,HiroshiD,TakahiroO,etal.Improvedlungfunctionbymeansofretrogradeflushincaninelungtransplan-tationwithnon-heart-beatingdonors[J].JThoracCardio-vascSurg,2003,125(6):901-906.[3]SteenS,SjobergT,PierreL,etal.Transplantationoflu-ngsfromanon-heart-beatingdonor[J].Lancet,2001,357(9259):825-829.[4]BoglioneMM,MorandiniMA,BarrenecheaME,etal.Pre-arrestheparinizationandventilationduringwarmischemiapreserveslungfuncioninnon-heart-beatingdonors[J].JPediatrSurg,1999,34(12):1805-1809.[5]廖东山,廖崇先,李增棋.大白鼠无心跳供体肺移植模型的建立[J].中华实验外科杂志,2004,21(6):747-749.LIAODong-shan,LIAOChong-xian,LIZeng-qi.Establish-mentofthenon-heart-beatingdonormodelinratlungtrans-plantation[J].ChinJExpSurg,2004,21(6):747-749.[6]ThorstenW,UlrichFWF,AntoniaF,etal.Innovativepulmonaypreservationofnon-heart-beatingdonorgraftsinex-perimentalluntransplantation[J].EurJCardiothoracSurg,2004,26(1):144-150.[7]张新,乔晨晖,郅兴义,等.两套管一支架管大鼠肺移植模型探讨[J].医学论坛杂志,2006,26(17):25-28.ZHANGXin,QIAOChen-hui,ZHIXin-yi,etal.Two-cuff-one-stenttechniqueforratorthotopicpulmonarytranspla-tation[J].JMedicalForum,2006,26(17):25-28.[8]HausenB,BahraM,MuellerP,etal.Donorpretreatmentwithambroxolordexamethasonefailstoamelioratereperfusion357中南大学学报(医学版),2008,33(4)injuryinexperimentallungtransplantation[J].TransplInt,1998,11(3):186-194.[9]DateH,MatsumuraA,ManchesterJK,etal.Changesinalveolaroxygenandcarbondioxideconcentrationandoxygenconsumptionduringlungpreservation[J].JThoracCardio-vascSurg,1993,105(3):492-501.[10]WatanabeA,KawaharadaN,KusajimaK,etal.Influenceofoxygenininflationgasduringlungischemiaonischemia-reperfusioninjury[J].JThoracCardiovascSurg,1997,114(3):332-338.[11]SteenS,IngemanssonR,BudrikisA,etal.Successfultran-splantat
